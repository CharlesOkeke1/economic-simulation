/**
 * ================= AI DECISION FRAMEWORK DESIGN =================
 *
 * PURPOSE:
 * The AI should make decisions based on structural trends and risk states,
 * not on single-month values. All policy scoring must be influenced by
 * memory-derived diagnostics.
 *
 * -----------------------------------------------------------------
 * DECISION ARCHITECTURE (PRIORITY ORDER)
 * -----------------------------------------------------------------
 *
 * The AI evaluates the economy in 4 layers:
 *
 * 1. SURVIVAL (Political & Economic Emergency)
 * 2. STRUCTURAL HEALTH (Medium-Term Sustainability)
 * 3. GROWTH OPTIMIZATION (Strategic Expansion)
 * 4. ANTI-SPAM / DISCIPLINE CHECKS
 *
 * Each layer modifies policy scores before final selection.
 *
 * ================================================================
 * LAYER 1 — SURVIVAL CHECKS (Highest Priority)
 * ================================================================
 *
 * These checks can heavily bias or override normal scoring.
 *
 * 1. Stability Crisis
 *    Conditions:
 *      - stability < 35
 *      - OR stabilityChange negative for multiple months
 *
 *    Effects:
 *      - Boost Security score
 *      - Boost Subsidy score
 *      - Penalize Austerity
 *      - Penalize Raise Taxes (unless deficit is extreme)
 *
 *
 * 2. Persistent Deficit
 *    Conditions:
 *      - deficitStreak >= 3
 *      - OR rollingProfit12M < 0
 *
 *    Effects:
 *      - Penalize Infrastructure & Education
 *      - Boost Raise Taxes
 *      - Boost Austerity
 *      - Borrow only if cash critically low
 *
 *
 * 3. Persistent High Inflation
 *    Conditions:
 *      - highInflationStreak >= 2
 *      - OR rollingInflation12M above threshold
 *
 *    Effects:
 *      - Penalize Subsidy
 *      - Penalize Market Support
 *      - Penalize Cut Taxes
 *      - Boost Austerity
 *
 *
 * ================================================================
 * LAYER 2 — STRUCTURAL HEALTH CHECKS
 * ================================================================
 *
 * These influence scoring but do not override survival.
 *
 * 4. Debt Pressure
 *    Condition:
 *      - rollingDebtRatio12M > 0.65
 *
 *    Effects:
 *      - Penalize Borrow
 *      - Penalize Heavy Spending
 *      - Slight Boost Austerity
 *
 *
 * 5. Growth Weakness
 *    Condition:
 *      - rollingGrowth12M below annual target
 *
 *    Effects:
 *      - Boost Infrastructure
 *      - Boost Education
 *
 *
 * 6. Growth Overheating
 *    Condition:
 *      - rollingGrowth12M significantly above target
 *
 *    Effects:
 *      - Reduce Growth Policy Bias
 *      - Slight Boost Fiscal Stabilization
 *
 *
 * ================================================================
 * LAYER 3 — OPTIMIZATION CHECKS
 * ================================================================
 *
 * 7. Infrastructure Underdevelopment
 *    Condition:
 *      - infrastructure < lower threshold
 *
 *    Effect:
 *      - Boost Infrastructure
 *
 *
 * 8. Infrastructure Saturation
 *    Condition:
 *      - infrastructure > upper threshold
 *
 *    Effect:
 *      - Penalize Infrastructure
 *
 *
 * 9. Education Underdevelopment / Saturation
 *    Same logic as Infrastructure.
 *
 *
 * ================================================================
 * LAYER 4 — DISCIPLINE / ANTI-SPAM LOGIC
 * ================================================================
 *
 * 10. Same Policy Streak
 *     Condition:
 *       - samePolicyCount >= 3
 *
 *     Effect:
 *       - Heavy penalty to that policy
 *
 *
 * 11. Cooldown Timers
 *     Conditions:
 *       - monthsSinceInfra < cooldownThreshold
 *       - monthsSinceEducation < cooldownThreshold
 *
 *     Effects:
 *       - Penalize repeated investment
 *
 *
 * ================================================================
 * DESIGN PRINCIPLES
 * ================================================================
 *
 * - AI must react to trends, not single-month values.
 * - Rolling metrics override noisy monthly changes.
 * - Streak detection is more important than spikes.
 * - Survival logic always overrides optimization logic.
 * - Expert difficulty increases weight of structural checks.
 *
 *
 * ================================================================
 * MINIMUM REQUIRED MEMORY SIGNALS
 * ================================================================
 *
 * - rollingGrowth12M
 * - rollingProfit12M
 * - rollingDebtRatio12M
 * - rollingInflation12M
 * - deficitStreak
 * - highInflationStreak
 * - stabilityChange
 * - samePolicyCount
 *
 *
 * ================================================================
 * FINAL OBJECTIVE
 * ================================================================
 *
 * The AI should:
 *
 * 1. Protect stability first.
 * 2. Protect fiscal sustainability second.
 * 3. Stimulate growth intelligently.
 * 4. Avoid repeating itself.
 *
 * If implemented correctly, AI behavior will appear strategic,
 * not random.
 *
 * ================================================================
 */
